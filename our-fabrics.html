Cleaning, Care & Protection

All of our fabrics have one of two industry Cleaning Codes. Hover over any of the samples on this page to determing the code, then see below for care instructions for that fabric.

Cleaning Code "W"

Clean only with water-based shampoo or foam upholstery cleaner. Do not over wet. Do not use solvents to spot clean. Pile fabrics may require brushing to restore appearance. Cushion covers should not be removed and laundered.

Cleaning Code "S"

Clean only with dry cleaning solvent. Do not saturate. Do not use water. Pile fabrics may require brushing to restore appearance. Cushion covers should not be removed and dry cleaned.
