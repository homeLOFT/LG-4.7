SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA LOVE

As a California company, we love to take care of locals.

Location, Location, Location

Since 2002, we've been shipping our leather furniture to customers all over the U.S. and Canada. We specialize in the best of the best when it comes to leather furniture, and produce our popular styles right here in California. We don't operate traditional retail showrooms, but we keep a few of our most popular styles hanging around our offices in Carlsbad, CA., so if you're in the area and just want to kick the tires before making your decision, schedule an appointment to visit our offices and we'll show you around!

Extra Savings

Our website prices include free White Glove Delivery to customers around the U.S. Our average nationwide shipping cost is much higher than the cost to deliver in Southern California, so we'll pass that savings on to you.

Faster Delivery

Customers around the U.S. can expect approximately 10-14 business days for delivery once furniture completes production. In Southern California, we can have your furniture in your home in less than half that time.

Service

With our factory located right here in Southern California, servicing our So-Cal customers is a piece of cake. We'll be here for you before, during AND after the sale.

Available to view

Current collections represented in our Carlsbad location. Even if you don't see the style you're after, many of our collections share the same specs so it may be useful to check out a similar style in order to determine build quality, seating feel, dimensions, etc. We'll have leather samples available, along with other option samples, such as nail head trim, wood finishes, etc.
