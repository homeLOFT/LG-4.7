First Time Here?

We make shopping for leather furniture easy and affordable. Our specialty is delivering leather furniture direct to your home, anywhere in the United States, but our commitment to customer satisfaction is what sets us apart.

We're the experts on leather sofas, leather chairs, and leather sectional sofas, and our trained specialists will take the time to educate you about our products. Our complimentary swatches allow to get a true feel of our leathers, and to make an informed purchasing decision.

We're proud to have thousands of satisfied customers enjoying our leather furniture across the country, and in countries as far away as Japan.

Worry Free Delivery

Nobody has more experience shipping leather furniture than LeatherGroups.com

Every leather furniture order we ship is unwrapped and pre-inspected by our carriers when it's picked up, to ensure that when our leather sofas arrive they're just as they should be. Your order is fully insured at no cost, and a team of trained delivery professionals will install your new leather furniture in the room of your choice.

Right now, there's no cost to you for this service!

If something isn't right, we make it right. We've always been committed to making our customers happy, and know that our success depends upon it.

Real Value

Our long standing relationships with some of the best manufacturers in the business allows us to offer high quality discount leather furniture at up to 70% off retail prices.
