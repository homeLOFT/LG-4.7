Whether you are looking for a natural, distressed leather or a more playful, consistent color you've come to the right place

Full Grain, Full Aniline Leather

Dyed all the way through, our Aniline Leathers exhibit all of the natural character of the Italian finished hides. Full Grain leathers are un-corrected, and the scars and natural markings found throughout are part what our customers love about these leathers. Unlike Pigmenting leather, Aniline dyeing does not cover the natural shading variation of the hide, resulting in fantastic color variation and shading perfect for that rustic, or organic contemporary leather vibe. These leathers scratch, mark and distress, creating an antiqued patina that just gets better with time.
