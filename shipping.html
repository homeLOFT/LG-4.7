SHIPPING

Free Premium White Glove Delivery on just about everything!

Experience Matters

We've been shipping Leather Furniture nationwide since 2002 and have developed long standing relationships with key Delivery Partners. We have the experience, and the logistics in place to get your furniture delivered right.

Free White Glove Delivery
BEST

Takes approximately 10-14 business days (about 3 weeks on a calendar). Your order travels in a furniture-only environment and is unwrapped and installed in the room of your choice.

Free Curbside Freight Delivery
FASTEST

Available on request, this delivery method moves faster but will require you to have friends or family on hand to help get the furniture into your home. Great when you need it faster.

Parcel Delivery
SMALL ITEMS

If you're purchasing an ottoman by itself or one of our leather care products, we'll ship your order via UPS or Fedex. Leather Care Products are the only products with a small shipping charge.

  Delivery timing estimates cannot be guaranteed. Some regions and/or times of year can require additional time. See our Full Terms here

White Glove Delivery

Your order is unwrapped and pre-inspected by our provider to ensure it is defect free, prior to being re-wrapped delivered to you. The delivery team is present for the final inspection and loading of the furniture onto the truck. This meticulous process ensures the absolute highest percentage of first time deliveries without problems or complaints. Your furniture will be unwrapped, installed, and packaging debris removed.

Scheduling

Scheduling is handled directly with you by our delivery company anywhere from several days to a week prior to delivery, They will schedule a 2-4 hour window but can give you the option of a call 30-60 minutes prior to delivery if you will need to run home to meet them. Please be sure that you are present for the delivery or you may incur additional fees for re-delivery.

Damaged Items

We take every possible precaution to avoid any damaged orders upon delivery, but it can happen. Don't panic! Our company stands behind our product and will do whatever it takes to remedy the situation for you. We can dispatch a technician anywhere in the US and most of Canada to resolve any minor issues right there in your home so you don't have to wait to start enjoying your furniture. Just note any issues on the delivery paperwork and give us a call. You will also have the option to have your furniture taken back to a facility for restoration to first quality condition and re-delivery.

Full Grain Leather

Full Grain, Aniline Leather is not corrected, meaning that scars and natural markings are not "corrected" or sanded away as with Corrected Top Grain leather. Natural scars and markings are celebrated as part of the natural beauty of Full Grain leather. Unless a scar affects comfort or is affecting the structural integrity of the leather, we will not repair, issue credits, replace parts or replace furniture for natural scars on full grain leather furniture.

Shipping to Canada

Contact us using the form below for a quote on additional shipping charges to Canada. Delivery is free for customers in the United States, and for customers in Canada we'll only charge for any added cost above our average US delivery cost. Because our White Glove carriers do not cross international borders, deliveries to Canadian customers will be shipped via Common Freight Carriers. Delivery will be curbside, and because of this, arrangements will need to be made by the customer to bring the items into the home.

Scroll down for complete info about shipping to Canada, including our Shipping Quote & Customs Broker Referral Request Form

Customs Fees, Taxes & Duties

For Our Collections, made here in America, only the first two apply.

Customs Fees

This fee is charged by a Customs Broker for processing the order through customs when it crosses the Canadian border.

Canadian Sales Tax

The Customs Broker will also collect the applicable Canadian Sales Tax. Expect to pay the same tax rate as if you were buying in the town we're delivering to.

Duties

Our Collections are built in the US and not subject to duties. Duties may be assessed on some Guest Brand products if they were not produced in North America.

Place an Order from Canada

There are only 2 things you need to know to get an order in with us. Feel free to contact us at 1(877)888-7632 if you need assistance.

Customs Quote

If you've used a Customs Broker in the past, or know of one, just give them a call and let them know what you're purchasing, the purchase price and the country of origin. If you need us to refer you to a good one, use our Shipping Quote & Customs Broker Referral Form and we'll be happy to help out.

Place Your Order!

Once you've received a Shipping Quote and determined Customs Fees and Candian Taxes, order on the site and we'll add the additional shipping cost quoted during our Detailed Confirmation process with you after you order. Any applicable Fees and Taxes are collected separately, directly by the Customs Broker.
